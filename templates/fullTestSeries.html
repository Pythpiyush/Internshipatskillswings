What to Expect

Format

  • The Speaking module lasts for 11-14 minutes and consists of an introduction section plus three parts.
  • The Speaking module is the same for both the Academic and General Training verisons of the Test.
  • The linguistic challenge of the test increases with each part.

Part Timing Content
Introduction 30 Seconds Name, Nationality, ID check.
Part 1: Interview 4-5 minutes 2 or 3 unrelated and short, simple conversations about your personal preferences or experience.
Part 2: Long Turn 3-4 minutes 1 minute of preparation time. 1-2 minute talk, on a topic which draws on your personal experience. There may be one or two follow-up questions.
Part 3: Discussion 4-5 minutes A more detailed discussion related to the topic of the long turn in part 2.
